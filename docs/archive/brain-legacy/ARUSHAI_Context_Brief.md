## 4. FMI v1 — Full Feature Specification

### Context
MVPL manufactures chicken feed — 5-floor factory, PLC-monitored, mixing up to 48 raw ingredients, producing 50kg bags for 470+ farms. 6,000 tons/month, 200 tons/day. Today runs on Excel + WhatsApp. On-site quality lab with 5-6 hour test turnaround.

### Core Honesty Principle
FMI catches errors at point of entry — not prevents humans from making them. Cross-validation across 3 data points simultaneously.

### Roles
Mill Worker · Shift Supervisor · Nutritionist · Store Manager · Maintenance Technician · Purchase Manager · Finance · MD · Lab Technician

---

### Feature 1 — Recipe / Formula Master
Locked digital library. Up to 48 ingredients per formula. Version history preserved. Multiple active formulas simultaneously. Foundation for all features.

### Feature 2 — Production Logging
Supervisor logs formula + bags produced. System calculates expected consumption. Cross-validates against available stock before saving.

### Feature 3 — Raw Material Consumption Logging
Pre-filled expected consumption shown. Worker confirms or corrects. Deviation beyond tolerance requires reason. Unverified entries marked differently.

### Feature 4 — Inventory / Stock Management
Real-time stock updated on every production log. Delivery logging with supplier details. Multiple warehouse locations. Periodic physical count with audit trail.

### Feature 5 — Low Stock Alerts & Purchase Triggers
Days-remaining = current stock ÷ 7-day average consumption. Tiered alerts: 5-day green, 3-day amber, 1-day red. Pending orders logged to prevent double-ordering.

### Feature 6 — Machine Breakdown Tracker
Worker logs: machine, problem, photo, voice note, timestamp. Immediate alerts to supervisor and technician. Escalation after 30 min unacknowledged. MD sees after 1 hour.

**PLC Integration (NEW):** MVPL uses PLC for 5-floor feed mill monitoring. FMI v1 uses human logging. FMI v2 connects directly to PLC via OPC-UA protocol — machine signals raise tickets automatically. Architecture is IoT-ready from day one. Need: PLC brand and model from MVPL.

### Feature 7 — Shift Reports & Daily Summary
Automated summaries. Role-appropriate versions. MD sees 5-line condensed dashboard. Supervisor sign-off required to close shift.

### Feature 8 — Deviation Intelligence (AI Layer)
- Gradual drift detection — week-on-week trend
- Cross-event correlation — machine runtime vs consumption anomaly
- Cost impact narrative — weekly ₹ excess calculation
- Predictive breakdown — learns failure patterns, alerts before breakdown

### Feature 9 — Role-Based Access Control
8 roles with specific access permissions. Role-based onboarding.

### Feature 10 — Audit Trail
Every entry permanently recorded. Cannot be deleted — only corrected with reason. Legal protection. Excel data importable as legacy/unverified.

### Feature 11 — Lab Results & AI Formula Intelligence (NEW)
MVPL has on-site quality lab. Every batch sample tested. Results take 5-6 hours. Currently in physical register disconnected from production data.

**Level 1 — Immediate:**
Lab results entered into FMI the moment ready. System cross-references against formula + consumption + production conditions. Dr. Anand receives diagnosis not just numbers. Every batch has complete traceable story.

**Level 2 — Medium term (6-12 months data):**
AI builds supplier quality profiles. Seasonal variation patterns. Formula adjustment recommendations based on historical supplier performance.

**Level 3 — Long term (when Module 2 live):**
Formula performance connected to actual farm FCR outcomes. AI correlates formula versions to bird performance across 470 farms. Cost-optimised formula suggestions when ingredient prices change.

**Ask MVPL:** Are lab results currently digital or physical register?

### Feature 12 — GUTWATCH Farm Health Intelligence (NEW — Planned)
Inspired by Tulasi's Broiler Gut Health Framework — automated version.
Monitors 5 health indicators continuously across all contract farms:
feed intake deviation, water:feed ratio, litter moisture, body weight
uniformity (CV%), and undigested feed detection.
Generates daily Red/Amber/Green gut health score per farm.
MD dashboard shows all farms colour-coded on map.
Phase 1: Manual Field App data entry with automated deviation calculation.
Phase 2: IoT sensors for real-time automated monitoring.
Replaces Tulasi's manual Wednesday checklist with 24/7 AI monitoring.
